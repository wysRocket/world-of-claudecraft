Craft outputs and weapons

Each material craft pairs a durable main line with a consumable or enhancement sideline, so every archetype reaches both permanent and consumable items. Weapon-making is split four ways, so every weapon class has a maker and no single craft monopolizes weapons.

Bows, staves, and shields find homes among the ten: bows to Leatherworking (the bowyer-fletcher), shields to Armorcrafting, magical staves and wands to Enchanting, mechanical ranged (crossbows, firearms) to Engineering, metal melee to Weaponcrafting. Jewelcrafting is the one pure-permanent craft, but it only ever pairs with Enchanting or Weaponcrafting, both of which carry consumables, so no archetype is left without both.
